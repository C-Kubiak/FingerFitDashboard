# FingerFit Dashboard

A secure web dashboard for viewing your FingerFit gameplay statistics.

**Live Site:** [https://c-kubiak.github.io/FingerFitDashboard/](https://c-kubiak.github.io/FingerFitDashboard/)

## How to Use

### Sign In

1. Visit the [FingerFit Dashboard](https://c-kubiak.github.io/FingerFitDashboard/)
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Sign In"

### View Your Stats

Once logged in, your gameplay statistics will be automatically displayed on the dashboard. The stats are organized in an easy-to-read format showing all your gameplay metrics.

### Sign Out

Click the "Logout" button in the top right corner to sign out of your account.

## Features

- **Secure Login** - Your account is protected with Firebase Authentication
- **Stats Dashboard** - View all your gameplay statistics in one place
- **Mobile Friendly** - Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- **Privacy Protected** - Your data is secure and only accessible to you

## Requirements

- A FingerFit account (created through the FingerFit application)
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- An internet connection

## Troubleshooting

**Can't sign in?**
- Make sure you're using the correct email and password
- Ensure you have an active FingerFit account
- Check your internet connection

**No stats showing?**
- Your account may not have gameplay statistics yet
- Try refreshing the page
- Make sure you're logged in with the correct account

**Page not loading?**
- Check your internet connection
- Try clearing your browser cache
- Try using a different browser

## Security

This dashboard implements industry-standard security practices to protect your data:
- All connections are encrypted (HTTPS)
- Your password is never stored or transmitted insecurely
- You can only access your own statistics
- Content Security Policy prevents malicious code execution
